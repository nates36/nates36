John Stockton

John Stockton, Jazz Basketball Legend







  • Born March 26, 1962 in Spokane, Washington.

  • Played high school basketball at Gonzaga Prep.

  • Graduated in 1980 after setting a city record for points scored in a single season.

  • Played college basketball at Gonzaga University.

  • Led the West Coast Athletic Conference in scoring, assists, and steals while averaging 20.9 points and shooting 57% from the field during his senior year in 1984.

  • Invited to 1984 U.S. Olympic team spring tryouts.

  • Drafted by the Utah Jazz 16th overall at the 1984 NBA draft.
